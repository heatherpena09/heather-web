Academic Background

I began my academic journey in PREP (2012–2013) at Early Learning School, where I consistently ranked 1st overall.

From 2013 to 2019 at Jose Rizal Elementary School, I was a consistent MTAP candidate from Grade 1 to Grade 6, earning top ranks every year and graduating as Salutatorian with High Honors. In Grades 4 and 5, I also became part of the Student Pupil Government, while in Grade 5 I discovered my passion for writing and joined the school paper, which I actively participated in until Grade 12. I also represented the school in various academic and journalism-related competitions.

In Junior High School at Lakandula High School (2019–2023), I was a consistent honor student from Grade 7 to Grade 10. I continued my involvement in the school paper and in different academic and journalistic contests, further strengthening my writing and communication skills.

For Senior High School at Colegio de San Juan de Letran (2023–2025), I pursued the STEM strand, maintained strong academic performance, and remained active in the school paper organization. My participation in journalism activities allowed me to continuously improve my skills in writing and reporting.

Now in my first year of college at the Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila (2025), I still aspire to join Ang Pamantasan, the university’s official publication, to continue nurturing my passion for journalism. While I originally intended to pursue Psychology or Medicine-related courses, I am currently enrolled in the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, where I continue to adapt, explore, and challenge myself academically while keeping my strong interest in writing and communication alive.

Career Goals

I aim to pursue a career that combines my background in Computer Science with my passion for communication and continuous learning. My goal is to become a professional who is not only equipped with technical expertise but also capable of adapting, leading, and expressing ideas effectively.

  • Strengthen programming and technical skills – Continue to broaden my knowledge in different programming languages and technologies, while also learning new concepts and tools I have yet to master.
  • Gain hands-on experience in technology – Apply my learning through internships, research, and projects that will give me real-world exposure in software development and IT-related fields.
  • Improve problem-solving and analytical thinking – Sharpen logical reasoning and critical thinking to effectively analyze situations and create innovative solutions.
  • Enhance time management and productivity – Develop discipline in balancing academic tasks, extracurriculars, and personal goals to maximize efficiency and output.
  • Develop strong communication skills – Strengthen my ability to express ideas clearly through writing and speaking, building on my background in campus journalism.
  • Grow in leadership and teamwork – Participate actively in organizations and collaborations to improve my leadership qualities while learning to work well in diverse teams.
  • Build adaptability and resilience – Embrace challenges, learn from mistakes, and remain flexible in pursuing academic and career opportunities.
  • Balance technical skills with creativity – Integrate innovation and creative thinking with technical knowledge to make meaningful contributions in any chosen field.
